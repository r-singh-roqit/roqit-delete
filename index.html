Delete Driver Account

To delete your driver account, please contact your fleet manager directly. This ensures proper handling of your account and associated data.

Important Information

  • Your driver license information will be permanently deleted
  • All associated documents and certifications will be removed
  • Your email address and contact information will be deleted
  • All trip history and performance data will be erased
  • This action cannot be undone once completed

Contact Your Fleet Manager

Please reach out to your fleet manager to initiate the account deletion process. They will guide you through the necessary steps and ensure all data is properly handled according to company policies.
